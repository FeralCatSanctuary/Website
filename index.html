The Feral Cat Sanctuary

...Feeding, neutering, and supporting Moss Landing's feral cat population for over 20 years.

Every cat is named and looked after.
Your donations help.

Moss Landing, CA, located on the coast between Santa Cruz and Monterey, is a quaint agricultural and fishing village (officially an "unincorporated area") in Monterey County.
The peace and quiet has tempted many people to abandon their unwanted pets for decades.

Local Animal shelters and services have moved to a "hands-off" approach to feral cats. Which means thousands of animals are spared unnecessary euthanasia, but the communities they inhabit are left to find solutions.

Sally Slichter has supported the Moss Landing Heights feral cats, a colony of about 50, for over 20 years. The costs of food, veterinary care and medication, spays & neuters total around $1,000 per month.
